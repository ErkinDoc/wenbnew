Bridging Science and Healing

With over 30 years of clinical experience across four continents, I've dedicated my career not only to treating patients but to sharing knowledge that empowers both healthcare professionals and those suffering from complex conditions.

My publications span the spectrum from highly technical medical guides for professionals to accessible, patient-friendly handbooks. Each book distills decades of practical experience, integrating Western medicine with traditional healing wisdom.

Whether you're a patient seeking to understand your condition, a caregiver supporting a loved one, or a healthcare professional expanding your approach—these books offer evidence-based insights and practical strategies.
